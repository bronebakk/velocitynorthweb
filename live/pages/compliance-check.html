What the compliance check covers

  • Cookies & consent (GDPR): detects your cookie banner / consent platform (CMP), Google Consent Mode v2, a real one-click reject, pre-ticked consent boxes, and tracking cookies set before consent.
  • Privacy & policy pages: checks for a privacy policy, cookie policy, terms, company registration number and contact details.
  • Tracking & tags: finds GA4, Google Tag Manager, Google Ads, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn, TikTok and more — and whether they are governed by consent. Plus an HTTPS check.
  • Accessibility (WCAG): reviews page language, title, viewport, image alt text, form labels and heading structure.

Why website compliance matters

Under the GDPR and the ePrivacy rules, tracking and advertising cookies need valid, opt-in consent before they load — and Google now requires Consent Mode v2 for advertisers in the EEA. Get it wrong and you risk fines, wasted ad spend and broken conversion tracking. Accessibility (WCAG) is increasingly a legal requirement too. A quick compliance check shows exactly where your website stands and what to fix first.
